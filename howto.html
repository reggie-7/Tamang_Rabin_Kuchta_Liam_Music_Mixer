Record player Instructions

How to Use the Music Mixer

1. Pick a Sound
Browse the available instrument sounds (e.g., drums, bass, vocals).
Each sound is represented as a draggable element on the screen.
2. Drag & Drop onto the Record Player
Click and drag any sound and drop it onto the label.
Once dropped:
The sound is loaded.
The record starts spinning to show it's active.
3. Control the Beat
Use the control panel to manage playback:
Play Button: Starts the beat — the record spins and your track plays.
Pause Button: Temporarily stops playback — the record stops spinning.
Stop Button : Fully stops playback.
Volume Slider: Adjust the volume of the current mix.
4. Repeat & Remix
You can drag multiple sounds one by one to layer and remix.
Hit play again to preview your new beat.
Tips:
Combine bass + drums + vocal for a full loop.
Use pause to edit your mix before playing again.
If you want to clear the track, use the stop button and remove sounds
